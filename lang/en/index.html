OUR WORK

Empowering cities and citizens with AI

ELBESTREETS

Reduction of street harassment frequencies and promotion of safety in cities using AI (artificial intelligence) technology.

We use data of places where people feel and don't feel comfortable to identify possible patterns in the design of the spots that can be associated to street harassment.
This serves as a tool to make city designs more inclusive for everyone.
